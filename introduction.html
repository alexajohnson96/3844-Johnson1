Introduction

This is the introduction to my Redux Website.

May 2017

I am a Public Relations major and a double minor in Professional and Technical Writing and Animal Science. I am hoping to ultimately do Public Relations/communications for a non-profit organization, but I am also interested in exploring other facets of PR such as lifestyle PR.

Over the course of this semester in this class, we have learned about digital writing and the VT-shaped student through our original website, our readings, and our video and podcast projects. Here you will find these projects, as well as an original design I made to support and improve upon the VT-shaped student model.

Here is a bit about my involvement outside of being a student:

  • I work at the Global Education Office in a position that reinforces the skills I'm learning in my major.
  • I have had internships in my field for the past two summers, and have another set up for this coming summer.
  • I am a photographer for the Collegiate Times.
  • I am in a sorority and hold a leadership position.
  • I studied abroad in London for five weeks last summer.
