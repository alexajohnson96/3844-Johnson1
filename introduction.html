Introduction

About me, my experience with digital writing, and what I hope to gain from this Writing and Digital Media course.

February, 2017 Alexa Johnson

This picture is of me and my dog, Jackson. He's my favorite.

My name is Alexa Johnson and I am a junior at Virginia Tech. I am a Public Relations major and a double minor in Professional and Technical Writing and Animal Science. I am hoping to ultimately do Public Relations/communications for a non-profit organization, but I am also interested in exploring other facets of PR such as lifestyle PR.

I have a little experience with digital writing from other classes, such as Professional and Technical Writing, and several internships. The summer after my freshman year, I worked as an intern for the Richmond SPCA in their communications department. I was in charge of designing a web page meant to educate students about our cause and the volunteer opportunities available to them. I used html code (very basic) during that internship for the first time. Since then, I have been able to use and expand my knowledge of html little by little. This class is definitely helping to strengthen my knowledge!

From this class, I hope to gain greater understanding and confidence in my writing and use of digital media. In today's world, digital media is vital, especially in the communications feild, so I hope to strengthen my skills and expand my portfolio as I move closer to beginning my career as a Public Relations professional.
